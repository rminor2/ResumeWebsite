About Me

A bit about me

Hi, I'm Ryan Minor — born and raised in Austin, TX. I've always had a knack for solving problems and thinking my way out of a tough spot, so when I discovered the Solutions Engineer role, it felt like a perfect fit. It lets me keep solving puzzles by digging into the technical side of every product I demo, while staying on the social side of the work — networking, building genuine connections, and getting out from behind a desk instead of staring at a screen all day. That blend suits both my strengths and my personality.

Outside of work, I'm an outdoors person through and through — and I could spend a while listing all the ways I like to get outside. More than anything, I'm excited about the people I'll get to work with next. Every new connection and opportunity energizes me, and I can't wait to see where my career takes me.

🥾 Hiking🚵 Mountain biking🎿 Skiing🏄 Surfing🎣 Fly fishing⛰️ & more

I'm a Computer Science student at UCCS and a two-time Solutions Engineering intern. Across those roles I've run product demos, led discovery conversations, gathered requirements, and designed solutions that solve real operational problems — not just write code.

I've worked directly with non-technical business owners, operations managers, and executives, translating complex software into plain, outcome-focused language. A lot of my work has been guiding manufacturing businesses through the move from paper-based processes to modern SaaS — meeting people where they are and showing them the path forward.

Before all of that I founded and ran a service business, which gave me a founder's instinct for what actually moves the needle: leads, close rates, delivery, and keeping customers happy. I bring that commercial sense to every technical conversation.
