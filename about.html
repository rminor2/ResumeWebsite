About Me

A bit about me

Hi, I'm Ryan Minor, born and raised in Austin, TX. I've always liked solving problems and figuring my way out of a tough spot, so the Solutions Engineer role clicked for me right away. I get to dig into how each product actually works and solve the hard technical pieces, then spend the rest of my time with people instead of staring at a screen all day. That mix fits how I'm wired.

Outside of work you'll usually find me outdoors. I could go on for a while about the ways I like to spend a weekend. Mostly, though, I'm excited about the people I'll get to work with next. New connections and new problems are what get me going, and I'm looking forward to seeing where this takes me.

🥾 Hiking🚵 Mountain biking🎿 Skiing🏄 Surfing🎣 Fly fishing⛰️ & more

I'm a Computer Science student at UCCS and a two-time Solutions Engineering intern. My work centers on designing solutions to real operational problems: understanding how a business runs, figuring out what it actually needs, and shaping software that fits. The demos and discovery calls are how I get there, not the whole job.

I work directly with business owners, operations managers, and executives, and a big part of that is making complicated software make sense to people who don't write code. A lot of it has been helping manufacturing businesses move off paper and onto modern software, meeting them where they are and showing them a clear path forward.

Before any of that I started and ran my own service business. It taught me what really matters when it's your name on the line: getting leads, closing them, doing the work well, and keeping people happy. I bring that same practical sense to the technical side now.
