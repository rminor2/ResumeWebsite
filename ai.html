AI-Augmented Engineering

How I build production software with AI

I treat AI as a technical coworker — not a replacement for understanding the code. It lets me move faster, ship more, and carry every lesson forward across projects.

The Process

A repeatable, business-first workflow

Every project follows the same disciplined path — from the business problem to reusable, documented knowledge.

01

Define the business problem

Start from the operational pain — who hurts, what it costs, and what "solved" actually looks like.

02

Document requirements

Capture scope, constraints, and success criteria as structured notes before any code gets written.

03

Design the architecture

Model the system, data, and integrations — then pressure-test the design with AI before committing.

04

Generate an implementation plan

Break the work into verifiable, bite-sized tasks with Claude Code as a planning partner.

05

Build, test & review

Implement, debug, and refactor with AI — then adversarially review the output before trusting it.

06

Document & store knowledge

Write down what worked and why, and save reusable prompts, decisions, and patterns in Obsidian.
